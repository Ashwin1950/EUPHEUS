B.Sc. ACCIDENT AND EMERGENCY CARE TECHNOLOGY

     The Accident and Emergency Care Technology course is a four year course which trains candidates in Emergency Medical techniques to become the first responders to all kind of medical emergencies. They are taught knowledge and skills to save the patients with various medical emergencies, within and outside the hospital.
