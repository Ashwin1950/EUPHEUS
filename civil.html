Why Civil Engineering?

  • The reasons to study BTech Civil Engineering will differ from candidate to candidate. Some of the reasons why most choose this field of study has been elaborated below.

  • Reputed profession: Civil engineering is a high demand field where skilled graduates are able to find open job positions immediately. It is one of the oldest and most reputable engineering divisions. Any student with the passion to excel in the field will find success soon.

  • High pay: Average packages for BTech Civil Engineering graduates are on the higher side for both private and public organizations. The work of a civil engineer involves initiating, planning, overlooking, and completing public infrastructure projects. Given the scale of work, the pay is also on the higher side.

  • Career opportunities: A Mechanical Engineer is able to pursue a diverse array of career opportunities. There is no limit to educational programs one can choose. From law, management to competitive examinations to get employed in Government organizations, the sky's the limit.

Course Details

  • Course Level -Undergraduate
  • Full-Form -Bachelors of Technology in Civil Engineering
  • Duration- 4 years
  • Examination type -Semester System
  • Eligibility -10+2 with Science Subjects
  • Admission Process- Entrance Examination and a round of counseling
  • Course Fee- INR 2,00,000 to INR 4,00,000 in public institutes INR 3,00,000 to INR 6,00,000 in private institutes
  • Average Salary -INR 3,00,000 to INR 5,00,000
  • Top Recruiting Companies- L&T, Sobha developers Ltd, Maytas Infra Ltd, Shapoorji Pallonji, Shimizu Corp, Jones Lang LaSalle, Jacobs Engineering, Alacrity Infosystems, TATA consulting engineers Ltd, Land Transport Authority, etc
  • Job Positions -Civil Engineer, Transportation Engineer, Water Resource Engineer, Structural Engineer, Environmental Engineers, Civil Engineering Technicians, etc.
