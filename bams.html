BACHELOR OF AYURVEDIC MEDICINE AND SURGERY (BPT)

    BAMS or Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery is a 5.5 years long graduate degree that covers the study of the integrated system of modern medicine and traditional Ayurveda.

Course Details

  • Course Stream - Medical
  • Course Level - Undergraduate
  • Duration of the Course - 5 years 6 months
  • Course Fee (per annum) - INR 10,000- 50,000
  • Eligibility - 10+2 in Science stream from any recognized educational board
  • Average Salary - INR 20,000- 80,000 in government sector and INR 20,000-50,000 in private sector
  • Top Recruiting Areas - Health Care. Dabur, Baidyanath and Himalaya firms, University of Madras, Kasturba Medical College, Manipal, Ayurveda, etc.
  • Job Positions - Lecturer, Teacher, Sales Representative, Pharmacist, Therapist, Scientist, Ayurvedic Doctor, Panchakarma Practitioner, etc.
