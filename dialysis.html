B.Sc. DIALYSIS TECHNOLOGY

     The students are trained to use various machines for dialysis help in the treatment of patients. In many colleges, the course is also known as B.Sc in Dialysis Technology and is focused on training the students to assist in dialysis treatments of the patients.
