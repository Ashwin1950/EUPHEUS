Why Marine Engineering?

  • Reputed profession: Marine Engineering is a high demand field where skilled graduates are able to find open job positions immediately. Any student with the passion to excel in the field will find success soon.

  • High pay: Average packages for BTech Marine Engineering graduates are on the higher side for both private and public organizations. This course demands extensive off-shore training, initially for 6 months, and upon completion, students will get certified as a Grade IV naval marine engineer by the Ministry of Shipping, Government of India. Given the scale of work, the pay is also on the higher side.

  • Career opportunities: A Marine Engineer is able to pursue a diverse array of career opportunities. There is no limit to educational programs one can choose. From law, management to competitive examinations to get employed in Government organizations, the sky's the limit.
