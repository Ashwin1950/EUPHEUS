BACHELOR OF ARCHITECTURE [B.ARCH]

     Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) is a 5-years undergraduate program that can be started after Class 12 with a minimum of 50% marks. This course is designed to produce licensed and professional architects who are authorized to do private constructions and government constructions.
