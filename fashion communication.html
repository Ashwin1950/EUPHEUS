BACHELOR OF DESIGN [B.DES] (FASHION COMMUNICATION)

    BDes Fashion Communication is a 4-years duration, full time undergraduate course which centers around the refinements of fashion designing. The basic eligibility criteria for BDes Fashion Communication is 50% marks in 10+2 examinations from any recognized board.
