BACHELOR OF MEDICINE, BACHELOR OF SURGERY [MBBS]

    MBBS or Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery, is a 5.5-years duration professional degree course on medical science. Out of the 5.5-years MBBS course duration, 1 year comprises a compulsory Internship that candidates need to do.

MBBS Course Details

  • MBBS Full Form - Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery
  • MBBS Duration - 5.5 Years
  • MBBS Fees - INR 10,000 to 6 lakh
  • MBBS Doctor Salary in India - INR 5-12 lakh per annum
  • MBBS Eligibility - 50% or above in 10+2 in PCM/PCB with English as a mandatory subject
  • MBBS Admission - NEET Entrance Exam
  • MBBS Job Profiles - General Physician, Medical Officer, Medical Coder, Dietician, Dermatologist, etc, and many more
  • MBBS Recruiters -Medanta Hospitals, Fortis Healthcare Ltd, Apollo Munich Health Industries Co Ltd, Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd, Cipla Ltd, etc.
