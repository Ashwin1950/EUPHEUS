BACHELOR OF BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

    BBM full form is Bachelor of Management. It is an undergraduate course comes up with a duration of 3 years. BBM course deals with a strong foundation towards entrepreneur along with the management skills which is required for the growth of any industry. BBM Course curriculum designed to cover most core concepts of business in-depth such as management principles, Economics, Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting etc. Eligibility criteria to take up this course is higher secondary graduates (10+2 or 12 grade) from a recognised board. This BBM course have greater impact on the growth of the business, the job scope is very high.
