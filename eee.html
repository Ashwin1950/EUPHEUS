Electrical and Electronics Engineering

    Electrical and Electronics Engineering is a 4-year under-graduation level degree aimed towards imparting knowledge and applying the concepts of electrical and electronics engineering to solve industry-related problems and issues.

Click here to know about the colleges offering this course.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Why Electrical and Electronics Engineering?

  • It is concerned with the study, application, and design of electrical and electronic equipment and devices.

  • This field is also preferred for students with a high caliber in Mathematics and Physics.

  • This out of the other engineering fields provide better and more job opportunities.
