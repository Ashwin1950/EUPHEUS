Textile Engineering

    Textile Engineering is a 4 years undergraduate program. "Textile Engineering" is a big research field of technology that deals with all activities of producing textile fabric & yarns which are involved in the process of textile manufacturing.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Click here to know about the colleges offering this course.

Why Textile Engineering?

  • The course can be taken up by students who have interest in the field of Textile manufacturing or because of their own personal interests and goals.

  • Career prospect: Excellent job opportunities are available for graduates in the course to start off their career in the field of textiles. Few job profiles available are Textile technologist, Process engineer, Quality control Supervisor etc.

  • Pay Scale: The starting average salary for graduates in the course ranges between INR 4 - 6 LPA which is quite good.

  • The areas of employment are excellent and most of the public sector industries like Mysore Silk Factory and Private industries like Fabindia, Bombay dyeing etc.

Course Details

  • Course -BTech Textile Engineering
  • Full Form -Bachelor of Technology in Textile Engineering
  • Level -Under graduation
  • Duration -4 Years
  • Eligibility -55% in 10+2 with PCM combination
  • Admission Process -Entrance Exam Based
  • Average Fees -INR 25,000 – INR 2,00,000
  • Average Annual Salary -INR 4 – 6 LPA
  • Top Recruiting Companies -Mysore Silk Factory, Grasim Industries, Bombay Dyeing, Reliance Textiles, Fabindia, JCT Limited, Bhilwara Group etc.
  • Job Positions -Textile Technologists, Process Engineer, Quality Control Supervisor, Medical Textile Engineer, Development Engineer etc.
