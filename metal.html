Metallurgical Engineering

    Metallurgical Engineering is a 4-year duration undergraduate degree program that offers study on the fundamentals of Metallurgical Engineering principles. The course offers theory and practical study on the science and technology of working and heat-treating metals and altering their physical characteristics.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Why Metallurgical Engineering?

  • Candidates can get a wide range of potential job opportunities in Railways, Metallurgical and Chemical labs, Hardware Companies, Airlines, Iron and steel companies etc.

  • They can easily get hired as a Metallurgy Engineer, Metallurgy Production Manager, Welding Engineer, Project Engineer, Assistant Engineer, Research Assistant, Maintenance Engineer etc.

  • They can also perform research activities in various reputed Metallurgy R&D departments and research labs to perform advanced research activities in the related field.

  • They can also join as a teacher or lecturer in the related field at the top-notch colleges and universities in India.

  • Even they can also opt for lucrative job options in various government sectors by clearing UPSC or IES competitive examination.
