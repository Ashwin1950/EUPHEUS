BACHELOR OF DESIGN [B.DES] (GRAPHIC DESIGN)

    BDes Graphic Design is a 4 years Design undergraduate course in which students are taught how to create and design visual content to communicate a certain message to a set of groups or the masses with the help of theoretical, business, and practical knowledge.
