MASTER OF COMMERCE

    MCom, or Master of Commerce, is a postgraduate course in the field of commerce that is particularly aimed for students who want to become an expert in different fields of banking and finance.

Click here to know about the colleges offering this course.

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 years (Full Time & Part Time)
  • Minimum Eligibility: Bachelor's degree (Commonly BCom) in a related field with a minimum of 50%.
  • Admission Process: University level entrance exam and marks scored at graduation level. The admission process usually starts in the month of May-June every year.
  • Top MCom Entrance Exams: Delhi University MCom Exam, BHU PET, Allahabad University PGAT, PU CET, CUCET, JUET, SKUCET, and other relevant entrance exams.
  • Top Universities: Delhi University, Banaras Hindu University, Allahabad University, Christ University, Mumbai University, Osmania University, NMIMS, University of Calcutta, Punjab University, and others.
  • Distance Education/Correspondence/Online: Yes.
  • Distance Education Colleges: IGNOU, SOL Delhi University, IDOL Mumbai University, Lovely Professional University (LPU), Madurai Kamaraj University, Pondicherry University, University of Mumbai, Karnataka State Open University, Mahatma Gandhi University, etc.
  • Specializations Offered: MCom Accountancy, MCom Business Management, MCom Banking & Finance, MCom Accounting & Finance, MCom Finance, MCom E-Commerce, MCom Marketing, and others.
  • Popular Courses after MCom: CA, CFA, CMA, ACCA, CFP, PhD, MBA, PGDM, PGPM, etc.
