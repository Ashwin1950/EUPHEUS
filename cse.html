Computer Science Engineering

    CSE is a 4 year UG course that studies practical and theoretical knowledge of computer hardware and software. This course lays emphasis on the basics of computer programming and networking while also comprising a plethora of topics.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Why Computer Science Engineering?

  • On completion of this course, students easily get jobs as a Software Engineer, Testing Engineer, Technical Support in various IT firms and MNCs.

  • Candidates having interest in an interest in grasping all the aspects of Computer Technology then they should definitely look forward to pursuing this course.

  • Average packages for CSE graduates are on the higher side for both private and public organizations.

  • CSE graduates will be able to get handsome salary packages ranging between INR 3,00,000 and INR 7,80,000 depending on candidate’s expertise in the field.

Course Details

  • Course Level- Under Graduation
  • Full-Form -Bachelor of Engineering (Computer Science and Engineering)
  • Duration -4 Years – Full Time
  • Examination Type- Semester Examination System
  • Eligibility- 10+2 from any recognized board of education having Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with at least 75% marks in aggregate.
  • Admission Process- Entrance Examination
  • Course Fee- INR 1 Lakhs to 15 Lakhs
  • Average Starting Salary -INR 3 to 15 LPA
  • Job Positions -Computer Engineer, Application Consultant, Network Administrator, Mobile Application Developer, and System Administrator etc.
  • Top Recruiting Areas- Retail Sector, Healthcare Sector, Manufacturing Service, Aerospace and Defense, College or Universities, Telecommunication Companies, Healthcare Sector, Agricultural Sector and Financial Service etc.
  • Top Recruiting Companies- Amazon, Microsoft, Apple, Google, Accenture, TCS, LG, HP, Oracle, and Wipro etc.
