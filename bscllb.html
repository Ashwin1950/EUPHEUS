Bsc LLb

    Bachelor of Science and Bachelor of Legislative Law (B.Sc + LLB) is a five-year integrated degree program that offers an integrated knowledge of science and law. It is divided into ten semesters and the maximum time duration to complete the course is eight years from the date of joining.

    Students who wish to become a lawyer and possess good communication skills along with a bent for science can opt for the program. After pursuing B.Sc + LLB, one can pursue a masters degree in law (LLM) as it makes you eligible for teaching jobs in reputed universities.

Course details

  • Full-Form: Bachelor of Science + Bachelor of Legislative Law
  • Duration: 5 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 45% to 50% marks aggregate
  • Admission Process: Based on entrance examination
  • Top Entrance Exam: CLAT, MH CET Law, LSAT India, KIITEE Law, TS LAWCET etc.
  • Top Colleges: KIIT School of Law, Mahatma Jyoti Rao Phoole University, Amity University, OPJS University, NIMS University etc.
  • Average course fees: INR 2,00,000.
  • Job Options: Criminal Lawyer, Legal Advisor, Judge, Legal Journalist, Government Lawyer, etc.
  • Average Salary Package: INR 6,00,000 - 8,00,000
  • Area of Employment: Trilegal, Khaitan & CO, Talwar Thakore & Associates, S&R Associates, Luthra & Luthra Law Offices etc.
