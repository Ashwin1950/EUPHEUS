Chemical Engineering

    Chemical Engineering is a four year undergraduate degree in engineering. Chemical engineering is a certain type of engineering which deals with the study of operation and design of chemical plants as well as methods of improving production.

Click here to know about the colleges offering this course.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Why Chemical Engineering?

  • Chemical Engineering is one of the established BTech branches in India as the graduates get hired by various government as well as private organisations.

  • Chemical Engineering offers bright career opportunities and BTech graduates can earn upto INR 3 to 7 LPA in India.

  • These graduates can opt for on field as well as office jobs. One can work at industrial plants, refineries, thermal plants if opted for on site job roles.

  • Graduates from reputed engineering colleges get great placements as international firms such as Schlumberger and Johnson Matthey participate in the placement process. These graduates are even offered salaries upto INR 20 LPA.

Course Details

  • Course Level - Undergraduate
  • Duration - 4 years
  • Examination Type -Semester system
  • Eligibility -Class 12th or equivalent with a minimum of 50% of marks.
  • Admission Process- National or State Level Entrance Exam Deemed Universities Entrance Exams Direct Admission Process
  • Major Entrance Exams- JEE Main, MHT CET, KCET, AP EAMCET, TS EAMCET, KEAM, Goa CET, WBJEE, UPSEE
  • Average Course Fees -INR 3 to 9 lakhs
  • Average Salary Package- INR 2.5 to 7 LPA
  • Top Recruiting Companies- ONGC, Coal India, Geological Survey of India, HCL, IPCL, Neyveli Lignite Corp., NALCO, TISCO, TELCO, Reliance
  • Job Roles- Chemical Engineer, Technical Service Engineer, Professor, Fire & Safety Officer, Manager, Chemical System Coordinator
