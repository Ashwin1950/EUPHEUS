AUTOMOBILE Engineering

    Automobile Engineering is a four year undergraduate program that offers insight and expertise on developing new concepts and procedures, manufacturing processes and design in the field of automobiles to formulate new models of products and refurbishing the existing ones with new age technology.

Click here to know about the colleges offering this course.

It is a full-time, 4 years course.

Why Automobile Engineering?

  • Students and aspirants who wish to build a career in automobile engineering have bright career options that help them secure better annual packages and incentives as well as incentives and rewards that help boost the morale of workers and personnel employed in this field.

  • Candidates can also work in the R&D department of prestigious automobile companies to develop and research on new technologies and skills to establish new networking systems and processes related to Automobile Engineering.

  • Candidates and Aspirants can also work on administrative posts such as managers and executives officers who work and supervise such processes and procedures to ensure optimum utilisation of resources and development of good quality products and performance.

  • Candidates can also rise up to directorial positions after gaining enough experience in the field and can become influential in their line of work in this industry.

  • Candidates who wish to become teachers and lectures can also work with educational institutions to teach other students about the laws and fundamentals of this course program

Course Details

  • Program -Bachelor of Engineering (Automobile)
  • Level -Undergraduate
  • Duration- 4 years
  • Eligibility Criteria -50% Qualified in 10+2 with Physics Chemistry and Mathematics.
  • Admission Process -Entrance exam and Merit-based
  • Average Fees -INR 10,000 to 4,00,000
  • Average Salary- INR 2-12 LPA
