BACHELOR OF DENTAL SURGERY [BDS] (GENERAL SURGERY)

    BDS in General Surgery is a 3-5 years undergraduate-level Dentistry course. It is the most popular course after MBBS in the field of medical sciences. B.D.S. in general surgery is the mandatory professional course for the students who want to become dentists at government or private hospitals of India.

    BDS in general surgery as the name suggests introduces and educates students about the science of dentistry. To practice the profession after completing the professional course of B.D.S in general surgery it becomes necessary for the students to register in the Dental Council of India.
