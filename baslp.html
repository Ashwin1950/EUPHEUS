BACHELOR OF AYURVEDIC MEDICINE AND SURGERY (BPT)

    Bachelor of Science in Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology or BASLP is an undergraduate course of 4 years. The course trains students to become audiologists and speech-language pathologists. They learn methods to treat, identify, and prevent hearing disorders in people. The students are also taught about hearing aids and technology.

    BASLP provides training to become skilled professionals. After completion of the course the graduates can work as Clinical Audiologist, Instructor at special schools, Clinical Supervisor, BASLP professor, Hearing aid Specialist, Rehabilitation specialist, and much more. One can also choose to become a researcher in the field of audiology or speech-language pathology.

Course Details

  • Course Stream - Medical
  • Course Level - Undergraduate
  • Duration of the Course - 4 years
  • Course Fee (per annum) - Minimum 20,000 rupees and a maximum of one lakh rupees in Government colleges. Minimum 50,000 rupees and a maximum of 5 lakh rupees in private colleges.
  • Eligibility - 10+2 in Science stream from any recognized educational board
  • Average Salary - INR 4 to 4.5 lakh per year
  • Top Recruiting Areas - Clinics, Hospitals, Universities, Research field.
  • Job Positions - Clinical Audiologist, Instructor at special schools, Clinical Supervisor, BASLP professor, Hearing aid Specialist, Rehabilitation specialist
